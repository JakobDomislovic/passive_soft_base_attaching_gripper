Overview

This repository contains the complete design models and figures for the SOBER gripper. All necessary files are provided to enable researchers and engineers to replicate the design and ensure reproducibility of results.

The repository includes:

  • Fusion 360 Files (.f3d, .f3z) - Parametric models for design modification and adaptation
  • STL Files (.stl) - Ready-to-print 3D models for prototyping, fabrication, and simulation
  • Technical Figures - Detailed views, diagrams, and schematics from multiple perspectives

The gripper is designed for robotic applications where passive attachment mechanisms are beneficial, providing a robust solution for grasping and manipulation tasks.
